# Knowledge Management Tools for Investment Research - Analysis Report

**Research Date**: December 25, 2025
**Focus**: Note-taking and knowledge management tools used by institutional investors and hedge fund analysts

---

## Executive Summary

This research analyzes how professional investors organize research notes, link investment theses, and manage knowledge. Key findings reveal a shift toward graph-based knowledge systems, bi-directional linking, and AI-powered platforms that combine qualitative expert insights with quantitative data.

**Key Insights:**
- Knowledge graph market growing at 36.6% CAGR, reaching $6.93B by 2030
- AlphaSense + Tegus merger created dominant platform with 240K+ expert transcripts
- Graph-based systems predict investment success with 84.7% accuracy vs 60% for traditional metrics
- Investors with high network centrality outperform peers by 2.3x

## 2. Roam Research - Bi-directional Linking for Investment Thesis

### Overview
Roam Research pioneered bi-directional linking in note-taking, enabling non-linear thought processes critical for developing complex investment theses.

### Company Background
- **Founded**: 2018 by Conor White-Sullivan and Joshua Brown
- **Funding**: $9M seed round (Sept 2020) at $200M valuation
- **Investors**: Chapter One, Spearhead, Metaplanet, Patrick Collison, True Ventures, Lux Capital
- **Philosophy**: "Writing is a tool for thinking"

### Core Innovation: Bi-directional Linking

**How It Works:**
- Built on graph database architecture
- When you link page A → page B, the link automatically appears on page B → page A
- Eliminates traditional file/folder constraints
- Enables discovery of hidden connections

**Investment Thesis Development:**
- Connect companies to market themes
- Link thesis points to supporting evidence
- Discover non-obvious relationships between investments
- Build interconnected knowledge graphs organically

### Investment Thesis (Why VCs Invested)

**Jeff Morris Jr. (Chapter One):**
> "The bi-directional page architecture helps express and rediscover ideas... Finding a great artist or software product that you love before they go mainstream is rare."

**Community Strength:**
- 1000+ developers hacking on Roam in free time
- "Cult-like" passionate user base
- Vibe similar to popular GitHub open source projects
- "Product will be built by the cult, not at investor dinners"

### Use Cases for Investment Research
- Building interconnected investment theses
- Linking macro themes to individual securities
- Discovering relationships between portfolio companies
- Non-linear exploration of research ideas

## 3. Obsidian - Local-First Knowledge Management with Finance Plugins

### Overview
Obsidian provides a local-first, privacy-focused alternative with extensive plugin ecosystem for customizing knowledge management workflows.

### Core Advantages

**Local-First Approach:**
- All data stored locally in plain text Markdown
- Full offline functionality
- Complete control and privacy over knowledge base
- Optional sync ($8/month) or self-hosted (Dropbox, iCloud, Git)

**Bi-directional Linking:**
- Every link creates automatic backlinks
- Promotes non-linear thinking
- Discovery of hidden connections
- Visual graph view of knowledge network

**Plugin Ecosystem:**
- 2000+ community plugins
- Highly extensible and customizable
- Active development community

### Finance-Specific Plugins

#### 1. **Alfonso Money Manager**
- Track and manage personal finances
- Local and cloud syncing
- Alfonso Query Language (AQL) for data extraction
- ApexCharts integration for visualization
- Mobile app synchronization

#### 2. **Ledger Plugin**
- Personal finance tracking in plain text
- Quick expense recording widget
- Auto-suggestions for past transactions
- Obsidian Protocol integration for mobile tracking

#### 3. **Account Viewer Plugin**
- Create accounting tables from Markdown code blocks

#### 4. **Hledger Notes Plugin**
- Seamless Hledger accounting integration
- Multi-currency transaction support

- Visualize research evolution with backlinks and graphs

**Investment Research:**
- Community interest in stock trading and investment use cases

### Key Differentiators

- Extensive customization via plugins

## 5. Knowledge Graphs in Investment Research

### Market Overview

**Growth Trajectory:**
- **2024 Market Size**: $1.06 billion
- **2030 Projected**: $6.93 billion
- **CAGR**: 36.6%

### Performance Advantages

**Network Analysis Research Findings:**
- Network position predicts investment success: **84.7% accuracy**
- Traditional financial metrics: **barely 60% accuracy**
- High network centrality investors outperform peers: **2.3x**
- Performance advantage regardless of portfolio size

### Financial Knowledge Graph Systems

#### FinDKG (Financial Dynamic Knowledge Graph)

**Key Features:**
- Open-source graph-based AI system
- Models global financial systems through dynamic Knowledge Graphs
- Powered by KGTransformer (state-of-the-art GNN model)

**Applications:**
- Risk management
- Thematic investing
- Economics forecasting

**Paradigm Shift:**
- Traditional models: retrospective, historical data-focused
- FinDKG: proactive, forward-looking intelligence
- Temporal learning capabilities
- Adapts to fluid market dynamics
- Anticipates disruptions and opportunities

#### ICKG (Integrated Context Knowledge Graph Generator)

**Integration:**
- Dynamic Knowledge Graphs (DKGs)
- Large Language Models (LLMs)
- Graph Neural Networks (GNNs)

**Capabilities:**
- Predict market trends
- Optimize investment strategies
- Improve risk mitigation
- Enhanced financial market analysis

### Enterprise Applications

**Relationship Discovery:**
- Build knowledge graphs from disparate data sources
- Identify new, complex entity relationships using GNNs
- Use GenAI to explain relationship implications for investments

**Emergent Information:**

## 6. Hedge Fund Analyst Best Practices

### Research Organization Fundamentals

#### Information Hub Setup
1. Set up centralized information repository
2. Identify critical factors and information sources
3. Create financial forecast scenarios
4. Develop systematic filing structure
5. Maintain clear documentation standards

#### Document Organization Best Practices

**Essential Documents:**
- Earnings transcripts and investor presentations
- 10-K filings (especially risk factors sections)
- Meeting notes and consultant reports
- Due diligence records
- Performance analyses

**Systematic Collection:**
- Clearly organized designated folders
- Efficient referencing structure
- Focus on management strategic priorities
- Track analyst critical concerns
- Monitor structural/secular threats

### Investment Memo Structure

**Format Requirements:**
- **Length**: 2-3 pages maximum (excluding exhibits)
- **Concise and actionable**
- **Clear thesis articulation**

**Required Components:**
1. **Company Overview** - Revenue generation and business model
2. **Historical Context** - Critical events summary
3. **Investment Thesis** - Explicit with valuation thresholds
4. **Key Thesis Points** - ~5 points justifying position
5. **Risk Assessment** - Comprehensive risk identification

### Data and Technology Tools

**Data Analysis Platforms:**
- Process vast datasets for trends and anomalies
- Real-time market data and analytics
- Advanced charting tools
- News aggregation

**Unified Analytical Environment:**
- Consolidated, time-aligned data environment
- Clean, accessible data
- High-quality feature engineering
- Realistic market behavior testing
- Live performance monitoring without friction

**Common Challenges:**
- Fragmented datasets across systems
- Execution systems separation
- Alternative data source integration
- Separate research environments

### Research Workflow

**Comprehensive Analysis:**
1. Integrate practical steps for informed decisions
2. Leverage detailed financial metrics
3. Consider industry insights holistically
4. Conduct thorough risk assessment
5. Identify potential pitfalls

**Independent Verification:**
> "Never accept management claims or sell-side reports at face value."

**Verification Methods:**
- Cross-check competitor presentations
- Consult industry sources
- Perform alternative market analyses
- Question investor presentation positives
- Uncover obscured real risks

### Alternative Data Integration

**Emerging Sources:**
- Satellite imagery
- Sentiment analysis
- Retail activity tracking
- Geopolitical trend analysis
- Unique market insights

### Performance Tracking

**Critical Gap:**
Many investors don't track performance of opportunities they "took a pass" on after writing memos.

**Consequences:**
- Loss of information on relative performance
- Missed opportunities for process improvement
- Inability to refine decision-making
